# Obrazz iOS App Landing Page - Design Guidelines

## Design Philosophy
Minimalist black-and-white iOS-inspired aesthetic with focus on typography, composition, and iPhone mockups. Clean, modern product presentation without visual noise.

## Color Palette
- **Primary Background**: #FFFFFF (White)
- **Primary Text**: #000000 (Black)
- **Secondary Text**: #7A7A7A (Gray)
- **Borders/Dividers**: #E5E5E5 (Light Gray)
- **Section Backgrounds**: #F7F7F7 (Light Gray)
- **Footer Background**: #F5F5F5

## Typography
- **Style**: Sans-serif, Apple HIG-inspired
- **Headings**: Large, bold, prominent
- **Body Text**: Light weight, spacious, line-height 1.6-1.8
- **Hierarchy**: Clear contrast between heading sizes

## Layout & Spacing
- **Section Spacing**: 100-160px vertical padding between major sections
- **Horizontal Margins**: Generous left/right padding
- **Container**: Centered, max-width responsive
- **Border Radius**: 20-26px for all cards and UI elements
- **Shadows**: Light, iOS-style subtle shadows

## UI Components
- **Buttons**: Black or white with black border, iOS-style rounded
- **iPhone Mockups**: Consistent proportions, soft shadows, prominent throughout

## Images
- **iPhone Mockups**: Required throughout (5-10 app screenshots prepared)
